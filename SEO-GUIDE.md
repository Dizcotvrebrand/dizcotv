## üöÄ Next Steps to Get on Google

### Step 1: Get Your Website Online
- Host your website on a domain (e.g., www.dizcotv.com or your chosen domain)
- Update all URLs in the SEO files from `https://dizcotv.com` to your actual domain:
  - `sitemap.xml`
  - `robots.txt`
  - All HTML files (canonical URLs, Open Graph URLs, structured data)

### Step 2: Verify Your Domain Ownership
1. Go to [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console)
2. Click "Add Property" and enter your website URL
3. Choose a verification method:
   - **HTML file upload** (easiest)
   - **HTML tag** (add to your website's `<head>`)
   - **Domain name provider** (if you own the domain)
   - **Google Analytics** (if already set up)

### Step 3: Submit Your Sitemap
1. In Google Search Console, go to "Sitemaps" in the left menu
2. Enter `sitemap.xml` and click "Submit"
3. Google will start crawling your site automatically

### Step 4: Request Indexing (Optional but Recommended)
1. In Google Search Console, use "URL Inspection" tool
2. Enter your homepage URL: `https://yourdomain.com/`
3. Click "Request Indexing"
4. Repeat for important pages (services, subscription, etc.)
